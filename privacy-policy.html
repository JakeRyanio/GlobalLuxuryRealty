By using our website or providing your information, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We may collect the following types of personal information:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

SMS/Text Messaging & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number and submitting a form on our website, you expressly consent to receive SMS/text messages from Global Luxury Realty. These messages may include:

  • Responses to your inquiries
  • Property information and listing updates

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any property or service from us.

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your mobile phone number or any personal information collected via SMS opt-in with third parties or affiliates for promotional or marketing purposes.

Your mobile number and the information you provide will only be used by Global Luxury Realty to communicate with you regarding your real estate inquiries and the services you have requested. We will not share your opted-in phone number with any third party for their own marketing use under any circumstances.
